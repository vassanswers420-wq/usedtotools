Why Track Your Daily Calories?

Understanding your daily calorie needs is essential for achieving your fitness and health goals. Whether you want to maintain, lose, or gain weight, knowing your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) ensures you make informed nutritional choices.

How This Calorie Calculator Works

This calculator estimates your TDEE using your gender, age, height, weight, and activity level. It provides tailored calorie recommendations to help you maintain, lose, or gain weight effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use this calculator for weight loss?

Yes. The calculator provides a calorie deficit recommendation (TDEE - 500 kcal) for safe and effective weight loss.

2. How accurate is this calculator?

The calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is widely recognized for estimating daily calorie needs. Individual variations may occur.

3. How often should I calculate my calories?

Recalculate whenever your weight, activity level, or fitness goals change to get accurate daily calorie recommendations.
