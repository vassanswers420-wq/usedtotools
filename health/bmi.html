What Is BMI (Body Mass Index)?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. It is commonly used as a general health indicator for adults.

How BMI Is Calculated

BMI is calculated using your weight in kilograms divided by the square of your height in meters. This BMI calculator does the calculation instantly so you don’t need to remember the formula.

How to Use the BMI Calculator

  2. Enter your weight in kilograms.
  3. Click "Calculate BMI" to see your result.
