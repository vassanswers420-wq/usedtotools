What Is a Position Size Calculator?

A position size calculator helps traders determine how many shares or units to buy based on their account size and risk management strategy. Proper position sizing is essential to protect capital and avoid large losses.

Why Position Sizing Matters

  • Prevents over-leveraging
  • Controls emotional trading
  • Maintains consistent risk per trade
  • Supports long-term profitability
