How GST is Calculated

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a consumption-based tax applied on goods and services. If GST is added, tax is calculated on the base amount. If GST is removed, the tax portion is separated from the total price.

FAQs

What is GST?

GST is an indirect tax that replaced multiple taxes like VAT, Service Tax, and Excise Duty in India.

How do I remove GST from price?

To remove GST, divide the total amount by (1 + GST rate/100).

Which GST rate should I use?

Most services use 18%. Essential goods may fall under 5% or 12%.
