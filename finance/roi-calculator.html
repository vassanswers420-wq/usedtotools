Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good ROI?

A "good" ROI depends on risk level and industry. Higher ROI usually indicates better profitability, but risk must also be considered.

Can ROI be negative?

Yes. If your return amount is less than your investment, ROI will be negative, indicating a loss.

Is ROI the same as profit?

No. Profit is the actual gain amount, while ROI expresses profit as a percentage of the investment.

Can I use this for stock or business investments?

Yes. This calculator works for stocks, real estate, startups, marketing campaigns, and other investments.
