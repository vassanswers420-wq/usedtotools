How Personal Loan EMI is Calculated

Personal loan EMI is calculated using a fixed interest formula where both principal and interest are repaid every month over the loan tenure. This ensures predictable monthly payments for better financial planning.

Since personal loans are unsecured, the interest rates are generally higher compared to secured loans like home or car loans. Understanding your EMI helps you choose a tenure that fits your budget.
