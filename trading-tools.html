Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Position Size Calculator?

A Position Size Calculator helps determine how many shares or lots you should trade based on your account size and risk tolerance.

Why is Risk-Reward Ratio important?

The risk-reward ratio measures potential reward compared to risk. Traders typically aim for at least 1:2 or higher to maintain profitability over time.

How does the Brokerage Calculator work?

It calculates brokerage charges, taxes, and other transaction fees to show your actual net profit or loss from a trade.
