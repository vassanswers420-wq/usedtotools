Frequently Asked Questions

What is profit margin?

Profit margin is the percentage of revenue that remains as profit after deducting costs.

What is markup?

Markup is the percentage added to cost price to determine selling price.

Which is more important: margin or markup?

Both are important. Margin reflects profitability, while markup helps determine pricing strategy.

Can I use this for retail pricing?

Yes, this calculator is ideal for retail businesses, wholesalers, and online sellers.
