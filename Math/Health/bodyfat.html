Body Fat Calculator

Estimate your body fat percentage quickly and easily using our calculator. Track your fitness, health progress, and achieve your goals with accurate results.

Why Track Your Body Fat Percentage?

Monitoring your body fat percentage is an important step in understanding your overall health. Unlike weight alone, body fat gives a better picture of fitness, helping you make informed decisions about diet, exercise, and lifestyle changes.

How This Calculator Works

Our Body Fat Calculator uses the U.S. Navy method, which is a trusted and accurate approach for estimating body fat. Simply input your measurements, and the calculator will provide your body fat percentage instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this calculator suitable for both men and women?

Yes, it works for both genders. Female users will also input hip measurements for accurate results.

2. How often should I check my body fat?

It's recommended to track body fat monthly or quarterly, depending on your fitness goals.

3. Can I rely solely on this calculator for health monitoring?

This calculator provides an estimate. For comprehensive health assessment, consult a healthcare professional.
