Calculate income tax under Old & New Regime including Health & Education Cess.

How Income Tax is Calculated

Income tax in India is calculated using slab rates. Under the Old Regime, deductions like 80C and 80D are allowed. The New Regime offers lower slab rates but no major deductions. A 4% health & education cess is added on final tax.

FAQs

Which tax regime is better?

If you claim high deductions, Old Regime may be better. Otherwise, New Regime usually results in lower tax.

Is this calculator exact?

This gives an estimate. Actual tax may vary based on surcharge, rebates, and exemptions.

Is cess mandatory?

Yes. A 4% Health & Education Cess applies on total tax.
