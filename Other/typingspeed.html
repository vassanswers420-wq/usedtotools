What Is a Typing Speed Test?

A typing speed test measures how fast and accurately you can type text on a keyboard. It is usually calculated in words per minute (WPM) along with typing accuracy. This typing speed test helps you evaluate and improve your typing performance.

How Is Typing Speed Calculated?

Typing speed is calculated based on the number of words typed and the time taken. Accuracy is measured by comparing the typed characters with the original sample text. The results update in real time as you type.
